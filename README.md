## Why Hausa AI Matters

Hausa is spoken by **70+ million people** across Nigeria, Niger, Ghana, Sudan, and the diaspora. It is one of the most widely spoken languages in Africa — yet it remains severely underrepresented in AI systems.

Most AI models trained on Hausa data produce output that is:
- Grammatically plausible but culturally hollow
- Correct in Modern Standard Hausa but wrong for regional usage
- Missing tonal nuance that changes meaning entirely
- Built without input from professional linguists

With 15 years of professional Hausa translation work, I am in a unique position to close that gap — not just as a data contributor, but as a linguistic evaluator, dataset designer, and quality benchmark creator.
